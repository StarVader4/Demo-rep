You are slime

You are slime is going to be an action based RPG focusing on you, a slime. You will travel through different dungeons, solving puzzles, and solving mysteries.

The slime can switch between elements that do different things. To not make dungeons complicated I want to have 1-2 elements per dungeon. Except the last one of course.

One thing that must be known is that one character will always appear in the game with a varying role.

They could be the villian, a boss, a playable character, protagonist, or just a random npc. You'll have to find them, they stand out.

You play as a slime with a sword, bow, hammer, or whatever I decide to put in the game.

I want the main character to be potrayed as the person playing while also being their own unique character, similair to Frisk from Undertale

A little pixel art of what the main character might look like.



Unnamed

As of now this project is unnamed but is one that I have wanting to do for a year now.

An open world, fantasy RPG where you can customize your characters. You have a team of 4 and can customize them all.

There will be 8 elements to choose from. Those being: Life, Fire, Water, Ice, Light, Shadow, Earth, and Wind/Air.

There will also be a mechanic that allows you to switch into your soul.

To keep it simple, you will take double damage but do more damage along with other various things depending on the soul.
